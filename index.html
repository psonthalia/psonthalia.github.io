    DeWaste

    I am a Co-Founder and the CEO of: DeWaste. At DeWaste, we work to reduce food waste in dining institutions. We analyze leftover food waste with automated photos of food waste and our custom algorithm identifies the food and then produces custom insights as to what can be done to reduce the food waste. We also produce a similar system to analyze food waste in the kitchen and production process. Our technology is currently used at UC Berkeley, UC Riverside, YUM Brands (KFC, Pizza Hut, Taco Bell), and more.
